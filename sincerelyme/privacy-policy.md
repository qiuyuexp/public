# Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

SincerelyMe ("we", "our", "us") provides a journaling and messaging experience. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## 1. Data We Collect
- Account data: identifiers from Sign in with Apple and/or Google Sign-In (such as user ID, email, display name when available).
- App content: recordings, letters/messages, and related metadata you create in the app.
- Device/app data: basic technical logs needed for security, diagnostics, and service reliability.
- Permissions data: microphone access (for recording), notifications (if you enable them).

## 2. How We Use Data
- Authenticate users and maintain account sessions.
- Store and sync your content across sessions/devices.
- Provide app features (recording, inbox, settings, notifications).
- Protect security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.

## 3. Services We Use
- Supabase (authentication, database, storage, and related backend services).
- Apple Sign-In and Google Sign-In for authentication.

These providers process data according to their own privacy terms.

## 4. Permissions
- Microphone: used only when you choose to record.
- Notifications: used only if you grant permission.

You can change permissions anytime in iOS Settings.

## 5. Data Retention
We retain data as needed to provide the service and for legitimate business/legal reasons. If you request account deletion, we will delete or de-identify your data as required by applicable law.

## 6. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with service providers strictly to operate the app, comply with law, or protect users and platform integrity.

## 7. Security
We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. No system is 100% secure.

## 8. Your Choices and Rights
Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data.

## 9. Children
SincerelyMe is not intended for children under the minimum age required by applicable law in your region.
