Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

Vellumark ("we", "our", "us") is a reading and annotation app that helps you capture pages from physical books, extract text and notes, and organize them into searchable Markdown. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

1. Data We Collect

  • App content: book titles, author names, page images you scan, extracted text, notes, highlights, Markdown content, and related metadata.
  • Subscription data: subscription status and purchase-related information provided through Apple.
  • Sync and backup data: if you enable iCloud Sync, mirrored copies of book metadata, page images, and Markdown files stored in your iCloud account.
  • Device and diagnostic data: limited technical logs and network metadata needed to operate the service, prevent abuse, diagnose failures, and improve reliability.
  • Security data: device integrity and anti-abuse signals used to protect the extraction service.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide core app features, including page capture, OCR and AI-assisted extraction, organization, search, and export.
  • Sync and restore your library when you enable optional iCloud backup and sync.
  • Verify subscriptions, restore purchases, and manage plan limits.
  • Maintain service security, investigate errors, and prevent misuse of the service.

3. Where Your Data Is Processed

  • On your device: Vellumark is local-first. Your library data is stored locally on your device by default.
  • Vellumark backend: when you choose to process a page, the scanned image is sent to our backend for OCR and AI extraction.
  • Apple services: subscriptions are handled by Apple. If you enable iCloud Sync, synced backups are stored in your iCloud account.
  • Service providers: we may use cloud infrastructure, OCR, and AI processing providers to operate Vellumark.

These providers process data under their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Optional iCloud Sync

iCloud Sync is optional and off by default. If enabled, Vellumark mirrors your library content to your iCloud Drive container to support sync and recovery across devices. Turning iCloud Sync off stops future syncing but does not automatically remove data already stored in your iCloud account unless you explicitly remove the cloud copy from within the app.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only as reasonably necessary to provide the service through hosting, storage, OCR, AI, and Apple platform services, to comply with law or lawful requests, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of Vellumark, our users, and the public.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide Vellumark and for legitimate business, legal, and security purposes.

If you delete content in the app, some deleted items may remain temporarily recoverable in a Recently Deleted state before permanent removal. If iCloud Sync is enabled, mirrored copies may persist in your iCloud account until they are removed or synced for deletion.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. No method of storage, transmission, or processing is completely secure.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You can keep using Vellumark without enabling iCloud Sync, manage or cancel subscriptions through Apple, delete books and pages in the app, and contact us to request access, correction, or deletion, subject to applicable law.

9. Children

Vellumark is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law in their region.
